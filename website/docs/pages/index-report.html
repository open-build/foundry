VMI framework

The Portland Operations & AI Index.

The index measures how companies adopt AI, manage operations, and build organizations that can operate beyond the founder.

Visibility

Dashboard coverage, KPI clarity, and real-time operating awareness.

Margin

Automation depth, AI leverage, and output gained per unit of effort.

Independence

SOPs, delegation quality, and how well the business runs without its founder.
