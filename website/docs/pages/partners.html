We accept partners who...

  • Cover a distinct lifecycle stage.
  • Operate with flexible, founder-friendly structures.
  • Have local presence and broader network capacity.
  • Align with the visibility, margin, and independence model.

We decline partners who...

  • Duplicate an existing partner role.
  • Require equity before creating operational value.
  • Offer only generic mentorship without a delivery path.
  • Cannot commit to the city-level operating model.
