Our Story

NullRecords was born from a belief that music should be owned, not rented. We release DRM-free, lossless recordings you can actually keep — no subscriptions, no algorithms deciding what you hear next.

Our catalog spans experimental electronic, nu jazz, ambient, and fusion — all created by artists who care about the craft. We also publish annotated editions of public domain literature through NullPress.

Everything we release is designed for collectors, artists, and music lovers who want to own something rather than rent it.
