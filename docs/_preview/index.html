About the Foundry Collective

A global network, born in Portland, designed to help founders escape the businesses they've built.

Why We Exist

The startup ecosystem is broken in a specific way: founders are told to "hustle" but never taught to architect their businesses for independence. Accelerators optimise for fundraising. Incubators optimise for demo day. We optimise for founder freedom.

The Foundry Collective emerged from a simple observation — the most successful founders aren't the hardest working. They're the ones who built systems that run without them. We call this the V·M·I framework: Visibility, Margin, and Independence.

Rather than being a single organisation, we operate as a curated partner network. Each partner owns a stage of the founder lifecycle — build, train, connect, operate — so startups get specialised support at every phase, without paying equity or signing away their future.
