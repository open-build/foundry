Partnership Criteria

We accept partners who extend the founder lifecycle. We decline partners who duplicate existing capabilities or extract equity without adding operational value.

We accept partners who…

  • • Cover a unique lifecycle stage
  • • Operate equity-free or revenue-share
  • • Have local + global capacity
  • • Align with the V·M·I framework

We decline partners who…

  • • Compete with an existing partner
  • • Require equity as payment
  • • Offer only generic mentorship
  • • Can't commit to the city model
