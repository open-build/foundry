Portland's Startup Foundry

First City Foundry is Portland's chapter of The Foundry Collective — a global network that helps founders build AI-native products, scale operations, and achieve true independence from their own companies.

We connect Portland founders with specialised partners across the full startup lifecycle: product development, operational architecture, community events, developer training, and investor readiness.

Through our flagship Portland Operations & AI Index, we measure how local companies adopt AI, manage operations, and pursue founder independence across three dimensions: Visibility, Margin, and Independence.

Part of The Foundry Collective

The Collective is a curated network of partners — not an accelerator, not an incubator — where each organisation owns a stage of the founder lifecycle. Portland is the flagship chapter, with expansion planned to Medellín, Nairobi, and Lisbon.

The Portland Operations & AI Index

A living survey measuring how Portland-area companies adopt AI, manage operations, and pursue founder independence. Scores across three dimensions:

Visibility

How well do stakeholders see what's happening — dashboards, KPIs, real-time data.

Margin

Operational efficiency — automation, AI leverage, and the ratio of output to effort.

Independence

Can the business run without the founder? SOPs, delegation, and decision autonomy.
